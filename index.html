Elevated Watch Points
Risks Unique to This Model
Charity Model

Collect-then-Donate Governance

Torch pools merchant fees, then decides when and how much reaches NGOs. Without independent audits and quarterly donation reports, regulators cannot verify that promises match reality. UK Commercial Participation Agreements are legally required.

AML / CTF

NGO Payment Flows

Pooled funds forwarded to NGOs across jurisdictions triggers heightened AML sensitivity. NeroPay must demonstrate to Stripe and regulators that it understands who pays, who receives, and how the donation component flows — including veto rights over NGO categories.

UK Regulation

Consumer Protection & Surcharges

A 10% fee framed as a card surcharge likely breaches UK surcharge rules. Even framed as "service fee + donation," ASA guidance requires non-misleading disclosures on pricing splits and donation mechanics.

Upstream Risk

Stripe & Card Scheme Dependency

Elevated chargebacks, consumer-harm concerns, or regulatory scrutiny at Torch level may cause Stripe to question NeroPay's third-party risk controls. Visa/Mastercard rules on honest merchant pricing apply throughout the acquiring chain.

Final Recommendation

Conditional Approval —
Proceed with Safeguards

01

Complete Due Diligence First

Financial statements, technical architecture review, compliance certifications, and legal dispute history must all be satisfactory before signing.

02

Robust MSA with RACI Matrices

Crystal-clear allocation of PCI DSS, KYC/AML, fraud, chargebacks, IP, and exit obligations. Engage specialist legal counsel.

03

Retain NeroPay Control Points

Veto rights on high-risk merchants, audit rights on Torch's compliance, emergency suspension, and brand-protection levers.

04

Donation Governance Requirements

Require formal CPAs with NGOs, quarterly donation reports to NeroPay, contractual audit rights, and full indemnity for discrepancies.

05

Phased Rollout — No Single Cut-Over

3–6 month phased transfer with readiness gates. Pilot capped at 20 merchants and £500K/month before scaling.

06

Live Risk Dashboard Before Go-Live

Implement monitoring framework before launch. Yellow triggers investigation; red triggers emergency governance and onboarding pause.
